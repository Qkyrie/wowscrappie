Scrappie says hi!

Welcome to Scrappie, your personal auction house consultant.

Updated for Warlords of Draenor! Scrappie will provide you with insight in the World of Warcraft auction house. It will teach you the basics of crafting, buying and selling on the auction house, to create an interesting revenue.

Scrappie is still under heavy development. New features are pushed each day, including new tips, statistics or entirely new functions.

Teaches you basics per profession

Covering each profession, we try to explain the when and the why of crafting, buying and selling.

Based on statistical data, using world of warcraft, wowuction and tuj, it will give you some easy-to-read tips and explanations.

Coming up, soon™!

We're currently working very hard to provide you with live feedback on when to buy, craft or sell anything on your server.

Depending on results of our number crunching, we'll be using various sources to provide you with dynamic tips for each profession.
